Available CDK Depict Options

  • Color Schemes: Color on White, Color on Black, Black on White, Neon on Black, etc.
  • Hydrogen Display: Minimal, Smart, Chiral Only, Explicit (show all)
  • Annotations: Atom Numbers, Bond Numbers, CIP Stereo Labels, etc.
  • Abbreviate Groups: Show common groups (Me, Ph, etc.) abbreviated
  • Zoom Level: 1.0x to 3.0x
